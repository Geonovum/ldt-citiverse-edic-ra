Find and Bind ()
> Being able to purposefully discover, filter, and retrieve relevant data, computational models, and visualisations within the digital twin. The find and bind function ensures that users can quickly and easily find the right data, computational models, and visualisations within the digital twin. Because a digital twin often consists of many different datasets, models, and visualisations, it is important that the digital twin is easily searchable.
