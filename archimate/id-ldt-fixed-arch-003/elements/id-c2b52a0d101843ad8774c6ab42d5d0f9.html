6. Ensure Sustainable Investment and Deployment ()
By pooling resources from member states, cities and regions, LDT CitiVERSE EDIC will create a sustainable approach to deploying digital twin solutions. This sustainable model supports strategic longterm investments that are essential for consistent, reliable digital services. It allows cities to make datadriven policy decisions on urban challenges, enhancing the resilience of the supply chain through stable, EU-led investment channels
